Introduction

This Project aimed to understand and analyze the bias in the media coverage of conflict happening in gaza. Using the swissdox API of the LiRi (Linguistic Research Infrastructure) of the University of Zurich, i got access to the data of the SRF (Schweizer Radio und Fernsehen) news articles in the time frame of the 7. october 2023 to the 21. may 2024. The specific time frame was chosen, due to the attacks of Hamas on Israel at the beginning of this new stage of the conflict.

Hypothesis

The hypothesis of this project suggests, that the media coverage is not only slanted towards more visibility for the israeli side, but also that the media coverage is biased in the way that it is more likely to use negative words when talking about the palestinian side.

Therefore the hypothesis of this short capstoneproject are the following: 1. The media coverage of SRF gives more visibility to the israeli side 2. The media coverage of SRF is negatively slanted towards the palestinian side

Discussion

The first plot is a word count of the three words “Gaza”, “Palästina” and “Israel”. Allthough it would be hard to make a conclusion on the media coverage of both of the sides on only this plot, it is interesting to see that Israel gets consistently more mentioned than Gaza and Palästina. This might imply that the media coverage is more focused on the israeli side. There is definitely more research necessary to make a definite conclusion on this.

The second and thrid plot both aim to investigate a portrayal bias in the media coverage. The second plot shows the word count of “Genozid” and “Krieg”. While the word “Genozid” is used very rarely, the word “Krieg” is used more often. This is especially interesting since multiple international organisations have stated that this conflict is to be considered a genocide of the palestinian people.

The third plot shows the word count of “Hamas” and “Palästina”. While Hamas is the terror organisation, palestine is the national state of the palestinian people. Even if hamas is the military force of the palistinian nation, it deligitimizes the palestinian cause to live free and have their own state.
